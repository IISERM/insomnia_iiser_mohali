About Insomnia
Cultural Fest of Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Mohali
"Insomnia" is a vibrant celebration of culture, creativity, and youthful energy. INSOMNIA has hosted artists and bands from India and around the world, some being The Local Train, Ash King, Astitva, Vivek Singh, DJ Seek, Dual VIbes, MTV Hussle 2.0 Rappers, Stand up Comedian Yash Rathi, Parikrama, Spunk the Rock Band, Swastik the Band, British Guitarist ‘Guthrie Govan, ’World famous Drummer ‘Gino Banks, Popular comedian Angad Singh Ranyal, Mohini Dey, Australian performer Murray Molly and MTV Roadies contestants. The fest has become a significant platform for students to engage, learn, and share their talents
